How we start

Saranata Arunodayak Community Foundation has a multi national staff and it was formed on December 2011.

With the slogan "සරණක් සොයන ඔබට සොයා ඇවිත් පිළිසරණක් වන සරණට අරුණෝදයක් ප්‍රජා සත්කාරක පදනම" we help people in need by doing many medium and large range projects.
